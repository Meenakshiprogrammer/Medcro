General
Practitioner

A General Practitioner (GP) is a medical doctor who provides primary healthcare services to individuals of all ages. GPs diagnose and treat a wide range of illnesses, injuries, and medical conditions, offering preventive care, health education, and routine check-ups. They manage chronic conditions, prescribe medications, and refer patients to specialists when necessary. GPs play a crucial role in maintaining overall health, offering personalized care, and building long-term relationships with patients. Their holistic approach ensures comprehensive medical support, emphasizing both physical and mental well-being.
